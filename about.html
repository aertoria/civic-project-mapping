WHO SHOULD USE THE COMMUNITYKC MAPPING TOOL?

This mapping tool is meant for any organization or entity working in community and neighborhood revitalization. This includes, but is not limited to neighborhood associations, civic groups, faith-based entities, local municipalities, community improvement districts, and even funding organizations. Individuals and businesses such as realtors, investors, general contractors, and those looking to buy a home may also be interested in using this map to find projects that are having a positive impact in the community.

WHAT IS THE COMMUNITYKC MAPPING TOOL?

The CommunityKC map locates and captures information on a range of community projects that are led by neighborhood organizations or ones that are done in conjunction with a neighborhood organization for the betterment of the community. The community projects highlighted in this map engage and empower residents. The community projects included in this map range in size from large projects like the redevelopment of a closed neighborhood school to small projects like a community garden or an art installation on a vacant lot. Information collected for each project includes start and finish date, location, collaborating partners, and general description.
